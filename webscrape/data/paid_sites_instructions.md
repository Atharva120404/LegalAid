# Accessing Indian Legal Data from Paid Sites

This document provides guidance for accessing legal data from subscription-based services.

## SCC Online

- **URL**: https://www.scconline.com
- **Access Method**: Institutional login or subscription required
- **API Availability**: May offer API access for institutional subscribers
- **Alternative Approach**: Contact their support for bulk data access options

## Manupatra

- **URL**: https://www.manupatrafast.com
- **Access Method**: Paid subscription required
- **API Availability**: May have API for enterprise clients
- **Alternative Approach**: Consider reaching out for research partnerships

## AIR Online

- **URL**: https://www.aironline.in
- **Access Method**: Subscription-based access
- **API Availability**: Limited information available
- **Alternative Approach**: Check if they offer academic or bulk access programs


## General Recommendations

1. **Institutional Access**: If you're affiliated with a university or research institution, check if they have subscriptions.
2. **Trial Access**: Many services offer free trials that could be useful for limited data collection.
3. **Research Collaboration**: Consider reaching out for academic or research partnerships.
4. **Terms of Service**: Always review the terms of service before scraping or bulk downloading.
